# Human Helper Job Description

## Title: Outreach & Operations Assistant (Part-Time/Flexible)

### Mission
Help grow our AI consulting business by handling research, outreach, scheduling, and client organization tasks—no coding required!

---

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Lead Research & Data Entry**
   - Find and list 10–20 target companies per week (using LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
   - Identify decision-makers, emails, and pain points
   - Fill out and update the outreach spreadsheet

2. **Personalized Outreach**
   - Use provided email/message templates
   - Personalize each message with company/contact details
   - Track sent emails and responses

3. **Social Media & Content**
   - Post about our AI servers on LinkedIn/Twitter (using scripts)
   - Engage with relevant posts (like, comment, share)
   - Help record or edit short Loom demo videos (optional)

4. **Scheduling & Follow-up**
   - Monitor email and Calendly for replies
   - Schedule discovery calls and send reminders
   - Send follow-up emails after calls (using templates)

5. **Documentation & Organization**
   - Keep pipeline spreadsheet and client notes up to date
   - Organize testimonials and update landing page/socials with new wins

---

## Requirements
- Strong written communication
- Detail-oriented and organized
- Comfortable with spreadsheets, email, and social media
- Friendly, reliable, and eager to learn

---

## Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Review QUICK-START.md and outreach-strategy.md
- [ ] Get access to outreach spreadsheet and templates
- [ ] Set up email and Calendly access (if needed)
- [ ] Walk through sample outreach and social post
- [ ] Practice updating pipeline and tracking responses
- [ ] Review follow-up and scheduling process
- [ ] Weekly check-in with founder

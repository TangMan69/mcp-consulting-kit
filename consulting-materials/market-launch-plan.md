# MCP Consulting Kit Market Launch Plan

## Executive Summary
The MCP Consulting Kit is a suite of custom AI automation servers (Business Intelligence MCP, API Integration Hub, Content Automation MCP) designed to streamline business operations, integrate with popular tools, and deliver actionable insights. This launch plan outlines the steps to bring the product to market, attract clients, and establish a strong brand presence.

---

## Product Overview
- **Business Intelligence MCP:** Natural language database queries for actionable insights.
- **API Integration Hub:** Seamless integration with Slack, GitHub, Stripe, and other APIs.
- **Content Automation MCP:** Web scraping and monitoring for real-time data collection.

---

## Market Positioning
- Target SaaS companies, tech startups, marketing agencies, and enterprise teams (50–500 employees).
- Pain points addressed: context switching, manual data gathering, inefficient workflows.
- Unique value: Done-for-you implementation, rapid deployment, conversational AI interfaces.

---

## Launch Steps

### 1. Product Readiness
- Ensure all servers are running, tested, and documented.
- Prepare landing page, pitch deck, and demo materials.

### 2. Outreach & Lead Generation
- Research and list 20+ target companies per week.
- Personalize and send outreach emails using proven templates.
- Engage on LinkedIn, Twitter, and relevant communities.

### 3. Discovery & Sales
- Schedule discovery calls, demo servers, and gather client requirements.
- Send proposals and contracts based on client needs.
- Close first clients and deliver pilot projects.

### 4. Client Delivery & Expansion
- Deploy servers, train client teams, and provide documentation.
- Collect testimonials and case studies for marketing.
- Scale outreach and add new integrations based on feedback.
